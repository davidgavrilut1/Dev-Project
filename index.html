ABOUT ME

WHO IS THIS GUY?

My name is David, and this is where my programming journey begins.
I'm a Technical Writer & Video Specialist at FintechOS. And I love my job, I love creating videos, especially for FintechOS!
But I've been working in IT companies for 4 years now, and I want to do more than just talk about cool products.
I want to be a part of the team that creates the cool products. I want to be a part of the development team.
To achieve my dream, I started taking action: I took a course on Udemy, and then, after finishing the HTML and CSS part of the course, I started building this website. This is a fully functional website, and its only purpose is to showcase my programming skills. And potentially, to encourage anyone else who is looking to embark on a similar journey!

FOLLOW ME ON

WEBSITE'S ROLE

The Dev Program website is designed to be a one-page website that is 100% functional. The only languages used to build it are HTML, CSS and JavaScript. No WordPress, no frameworks, none of that. It serves as a practical resume for myself, in terms of programming skills that I acquired during a short period of time. Apart from admiring its beauty, visitors can also subscribe to my newsletter, by clicking on the various Subscribe buttons.
